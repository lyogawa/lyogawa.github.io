About Us

We are a group of jazz lovers located in Chicago. We are dedicated to bringing all of you crazy cool cats the knowledge you need in order to find the best, and the snazziest of jazz clubs in Chicago. Led by our Senior Jazz Enthusiast, our chapter strives to learn everything about the Chicago Jazz scene. Check out the links to some of our favorite clubs at our Home page!
